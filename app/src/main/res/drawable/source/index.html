    Frequently Asked Questions

    How accurate is the food recognition? +
    Our AI model is trained on millions of food images and achieves over 90% accuracy in identifying common foods. For best results, ensure good lighting and a clear photo of the food.
    Do I need to create an account? +
    No! You can use FoodScan AI without creating an account. However, creating a free account allows you to save your scan history and track your nutrition over time.
    What types of food can it recognize? +
    FoodScan AI can recognize thousands of foods including fruits, vegetables, meats, prepared dishes, snacks, and beverages. It works best with single food items or simple dishes.
    Is my data secure? +
    Yes! We take privacy seriously. Images are processed securely and are not stored on our servers unless you choose to save them to your account. We never share your data with third parties.
    Can I use this for meal planning? +
    Absolutely! Many users scan their meals to track calories and nutrition. Pro users get access to history tracking and can export reports for comprehensive meal planning.
